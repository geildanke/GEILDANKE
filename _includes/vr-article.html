How you can use VR in the Web.

  • Cardboard

    The cardboard is the cheapest way to experience virtual reality. It is basically just a headset without a display made of paper or plastic, that you can use with your mobile phone.

  • Samsung Gear VR

    Another virtual reality headset is the Samsung Gear VR. You can use it with a modern Samsung Galaxy phone and there is also the Gear 360 camera for making 360 degree videos or images.

  • A Mobile Browser

    With a fallback solution you can easily provide a 3D experience on mobile phones. Looking around the VR space may be possible by rotating the phone or swiping the screen.

  • Oculus Rift

    The Oculus Rift is a virtual reality headset, that has an OLED panel for each eye. It works with a PC and therefore serves a better experience than a headset that works with mobile phones.

  • HTC Vive

    Like the Oculus Rift, the HTC Vive works works with a PC and uses two screens. It also comes with special VR controllers and laser position sensors to track the user's movement in a room.

  • Desktop PC or Notebook

    As for the mobile phone, in most cases you can serve a fallback solution for Non-VR devices like notebooks and desktops. Keep in mind that these fallback solutions depend on mouse or keyboard input only.
